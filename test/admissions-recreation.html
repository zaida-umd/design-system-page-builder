About UMD

Founded in 1856, the University of Maryland is the state's flagship research institution and a top-ranked public university. Our 41,000+ students learn from world-class faculty, work alongside cutting-edge researchers, and join a fearless community committed to making a difference.

1856

The year UMD was founded as Maryland Agricultural College.
