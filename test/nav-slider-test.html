About the Department

The Department of Computer Science at the University of Maryland is one of the top-ranked programs in the nation, with a long tradition of excellence in research, education, and innovation. Our faculty are leaders in fields ranging from artificial intelligence and machine learning to cybersecurity, programming languages, and human-computer interaction.

We offer a dynamic and rigorous curriculum designed to prepare students for careers in industry, academia, and government — as well as for the challenges and opportunities that don't yet exist.

"Computer science is not just about programming — it is about solving the hardest problems humanity faces, with creativity, rigor, and purpose."

Research Strengths

Our faculty conduct cutting-edge research across a broad range of areas. Current focus areas include:

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Cybersecurity & Privacy
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Programming Languages & Software Engineering
  • Quantum Computing & Algorithms
  • Computer Vision & Robotics

Programs Offered

We offer bachelor's, master's, and doctoral programs, as well as a variety of specializations and joint degree options with other departments. Our undergraduate program is consistently ranked among the top 10 public university CS programs in the country.

Graduate students benefit from close collaboration with faculty on funded research projects, strong industry partnerships, and access to the broader university research ecosystem including the Institute for Advanced Computer Studies (UMIACS).

Student Opportunities

Beyond the classroom, students have access to a rich network of clubs, research labs, hackathons, and internship pipelines with leading technology companies. The department regularly hosts industry speakers, career fairs, and open-source contribution events.

  • Undergraduate Research Assistantships
  • Graduate Teaching & Research Fellowships
  • ACM Student Chapter & hackathons
  • Women in CS and diversity-in-tech initiatives
