A Message of Purpose

Dean Amitabh Varshney joined CMNS in 2019 after a distinguished career at the intersection of computer science and visualization. Under his leadership the college has expanded interdisciplinary research, strengthened industry partnerships, and grown graduate enrollment by 18 percent.
